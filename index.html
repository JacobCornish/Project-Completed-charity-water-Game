Guide Drippy Home 🏡
Cross polluted rivers, dodge pollution trucks, and deliver clean water to communities in need. Reach the village to complete each crossing.

How to Play

Arrow keys / WASD on desktop — or use the D-pad below on mobile
Land on a yellow jerry can floating in the river for a +5 bonus ✨
Trucks and polluted water cost a life — you have 3 total
Reach the village row to complete a crossing — 10 crossings wins!
Difficulty
Slower traffic · 5 lives · 15 crossings to win
